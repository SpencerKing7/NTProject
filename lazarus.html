He does the impossible

Lazarus was a close friend of Jesus, along with his sisters Martha and Mary. When Lazarus fell ill and died, his sisters sent word to Jesus, who was preaching in a different city. When Jesus arrived at the tomb where Lazarus had been buried for four days, he commanded Lazarus to come out of the tomb, and Lazarus rose from the dead.

Our Savior has the power to do the impossible. Martha and Mary thought all was lost, but through faith in Him and by His grace, this miracle was performed. If He has the power to raise someone from the dead, just imagine the miracles He can perform in your own life.
