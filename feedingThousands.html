He multiplies what we have

Prior to this miracle, Jesus was attempting to retreat to a quiet place to pray to His Father and get some time alone. However, thousands of people followed him around the lake to receive miracles and to be taught. It was getting late and many people were hungry. All the people had to offer was five loaves of bread and two fishes. He multiplied what was given and fed thousands of people with excess food left over.

Jesus can do the same with us. The lesson I learn from this miracle is that He can take what we give Him, no matter how small or weak we are, and He can create miracles out of it. I experienced this on my mission when He used what little I could give Him, and we saw many miracles with His help.
